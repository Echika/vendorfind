Our Commitment to Sustainable Development

VendorFind is proud to contribute to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Our platform is designed to create positive social and economic impact in local communities.

💼

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

VendorFind promotes inclusive and sustainable economic growth by:

  • Providing free digital presence for local vendors
  • Creating opportunities for small businesses to reach new customers
  • Supporting entrepreneurship in local communities
  • Enabling decent work opportunities for vendors
  • Promoting local economic development

SDG 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure

VendorFind contributes to building resilient infrastructure and fostering innovation by:

  • Creating a digital infrastructure for local commerce
  • Innovating how customers discover local businesses
  • Making technology accessible to all vendors
  • Supporting sustainable and resilient business practices
  • Enabling technology adoption at the local level
